Lasagna

Lasagna dish

Lasagna is tasty and favoured by the people living at the Kenyan Coast

Ingredients:

  • Meat.
  • Rice.
  • Spices.
  • Salt.
  • Tallow.
  • Onions.
  • Tomatoes.

Cooking Steps:

  1. Cut the tomatoes and onions.
  2. Cut the meat into small pieces. Put them into a cooking pot and start boiling.
  3. After 1 hour remove the cooking pot from the fire.
  4. Put another cooking pot on the fire. POur the cut tomatoes, onions and the spices and add tallow/ Stir for 5 minutes.
  5. Add the boiled meat into the cooking pot with the mixture of the tomatoes, onions and spices. Stir for 5 minutes.
  6. Wash the rice and add it into the cooking pot on the fire. Stir for 20 minutes.
  7. Add salt and cover the coooking pot with a cover pan and put some charcoal on it. Wait for 40 minutes.
  8. Serve the dish and eat.
